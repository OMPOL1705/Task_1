Main Characters

ZORA HARRIS

Zora Harris is a biological scientist, that studies neutrogenomics, which is a fascinating field of science that studies the connections between the human genome, and nutrition. She is highly intelligent, and very meticulous.

DAPHNE KINCAID

Daphne is confident, assertive, and unapologetic about her sensuality. She is a powerful leader who yearns to be lead and commanded by someone who is even more powerful. She is used to taking charge in her professional life, but finds no balance in her personal life.

MIA JONES

Mia's personality is bubbly and vivacious, with a contagious energy that makes her the life of the party. She is outgoing and social, with a natural charm that draws people to her.

AMIRA SINCLAIR

Amira is confident and assertive, with a no-nonsense attitude that commands respect. She is a Sex Therapist who takes pride in her work and is not afraid to stand up for what she believes in.

DESTINY SOROS

Destiny's personality is magnetic and alluring, with a natural charm and charisma that draws people to her. She is fiercely intelligent and confident, with a sharp wit and a no-nonsense attitude that commands respect.
