Series Overview

Kittens After Dark is a daring and unflinching exploration of female sexuality, desire, and empowerment. It delves into the lives of six mature women who, on the surface, appear to have it all together but share the secret of being sex addicts. The series pushes the boundaries of their desires and fetishes, challenging societal norms and sparking discussions about sexual exploration and satisfaction.
